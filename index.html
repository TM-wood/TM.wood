Meet the Maker

T.M.Wood is a one-man workshop run by Toth Mathias. Every piece from the first sketch to the final coat of finish passes through the same pair of hands. The belief driving everything here is simple: whatever you make, make it as well as it can possibly be made. Not because perfection is ever fully within reach, but because striving for it is what separates something ordinary from something worth keeping.

Form follows function, but design is the goal. The aim is furniture that earns its place in a room not just by doing its job, but by being worth looking at.

Most of the timber used in T.M.Wood pieces comes from family woodland, felled, milled, and dried locally. It means the wood has a story before the furniture does. Every ring, every knot, every shift in grain is the result of decades of growth in a specific place, and that character becomes part of what you bring home.
