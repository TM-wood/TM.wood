Minimalist Cafe

Client: Local Coffee Collective
Year: 2024
Materials: Walnut, Brass, Steel

A complete interior design and build for a specialty coffee shop emphasizing minimalist design principles. The project showcases the harmony between form and function, with every element serving a purpose.

The Challenge

Transform a blank industrial space into a welcoming yet minimal coffee experience. The client wanted sophistication without clutter, warmth without excess.

The Solution

  • Custom walnut counter and shelving system
  • Strategic brass hardware for visual interest
  • Integrated brewing station with clean lines
  • Purposeful negative space throughout
  • Local sourced materials, family farm timber

Year: 2023
